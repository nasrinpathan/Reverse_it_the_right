    Problem Statement

    The common challenge when learning dance from YouTube or any video tutorial. “mirror problem”: when you watch someone facing you, their right side appears as your left, which makes following along confusing. Two solutions:

    Solution 1: Laptop Facing a Mirror

    How it works: Place your laptop in front of a mirror and watch the video’s reflection.

    Benefit: The mirrored reflection flips the dancer’s movements, so when they raise their right hand, you also raise your right hand.

    Drawback: Requires a physical mirror setup, which isn’t always convenient.

    Solution 2: Mirrored Video

    How it works: Use software or browser extensions to flip the video horizontally (many video players or editing tools allow this).

    Benefit: Much more practical — you can directly watch the mirrored version without needing a mirror.

    Drawback: Not all platforms support mirroring natively, so you may need third-party tools.
